Common Questions

What is Finder One? ▼

Finder One is our premium membership tier. For $199 a year, you get an automated bill-switching service, guaranteed extra cashback on all Finder rewards, and exclusive access to high-value product drops like groceries, flights, and electronics.

How do the limited drops work? ▼

Drops happen sporadically and are heavily capped. We might offer 500 spots for 50% off groceries. When a drop goes live, Finder One members are notified. Waitlist members get secondary access if it doesn't sell out (it usually does).

When does the waitlist open? ▼

We are gradually inviting users off the waitlist to ensure our concierge team can handle the automated switching. You can jump the queue by referring friends.
